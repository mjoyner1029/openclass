About me

Background

Meet Miles, a motivated and resilient young man from Oakland, California. Growing up in East Oakland, he spent his formative years playing football, showcasing his talent and teamwork on the field until college. With a father who serves as a police officer and a mother who excels in real estate, Miles learned the importance of hard work and dedication early on. His sister, now a successful lawyer, has always been a source of inspiration for him. As a proud Black man, Miles is determined to carve out his own path in the tech industry, where he aims to break barriers and drive innovation. With a passion for technology and a vision for the future, he is committed to making a lasting impact and empowering others along the way.
